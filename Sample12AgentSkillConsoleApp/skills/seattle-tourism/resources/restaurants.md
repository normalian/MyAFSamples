# Seattle Restaurant Guide

## Near Pike Place Market
1. **Pike Place Chowder** - Award-winning clam chowder and seafood bisque
2. **Beecher's Handmade Cheese** - Famous fresh mac & cheese and artisan cheeses
3. **Piroshky Piroshky** - Russian pastries and savory pies
4. **The Walrus and the Carpenter** - Oyster bar with seasonal small plates
5. **Matt's in the Market** - Pacific Northwest fine dining with market views

## Near Space Needle
1. **SkyCity Restaurant** - Rotating restaurant with panoramic city views
2. **Toulouse Petit** - Cajun-Creole brunch and cocktails
3. **Serious Pie** - Wood-fired artisan pizzas
4. **Poquitos** - Creative Mexican cuisine and mezcal bar
5. **Mamnoon** - Middle Eastern cuisine with local ingredients

## Waterfront Area
1. **Ivar's Acres of Clams** - Iconic Seattle seafood since 1938
2. **The Crab Pot** - Seafeast experience with communal dining
3. **Miner's Landing** - Casual waterfront dining on Pier 57
4. **Steelhead Diner** - Pacific Northwest comfort food
5. **Six Seven Restaurant** - Upscale waterfront dining at Edgewater Hotel

## Capitol Hill
1. **Altura** - Italian tasting menu with local ingredients
2. **Momiji** - Creative sushi and Japanese fusion
3. **Stateside** - Vietnamese-French cuisine
4. **Canon** - Craft cocktails and whiskey bar
5. **Nue** - Global street food from around the world
