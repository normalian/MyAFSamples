# Seattle Attractions

## Outdoor
1. **Discovery Park** - 534 acres of beaches, forest trails, and lighthouse views
2. **Kerry Park** - Best panoramic city skyline and Mt. Rainier views
3. **Green Lake Park** - 2.8-mile walking/biking loop around the lake
4. **Alki Beach** - Sandy beach with stunning views of the Olympic Mountains
5. **Washington Park Arboretum** - 230 acres of botanical gardens

## Cultural
1. **Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP)** - Music, sci-fi, and pop culture exhibits
2. **Seattle Art Museum** - World-class art collection spanning centuries
3. **Chihuly Garden and Glass** - Stunning glass sculptures by Dale Chihuly
4. **Wing Luke Museum** - Asian Pacific American heritage and culture
5. **Seattle Asian Art Museum** - Art from across Asia in Volunteer Park

## Family
1. **Seattle Aquarium** - Marine life exhibits on the waterfront
2. **Woodland Park Zoo** - Over 1,000 animals from around the world
3. **Pacific Science Center** - Interactive science exhibits and IMAX theater
4. **Seattle Great Wheel** - 175-foot Ferris wheel on Pier 57
5. **Museum of Flight** - Largest air and space museum on the West Coast

## Food & Markets
1. **Pike Place Market** - Iconic farmers market since 1907
2. **Ballard Farmers Market** - Local produce, crafts, and street food
3. **International District** - Diverse Asian cuisine and bubble tea
4. **Capitol Hill** - Trendy restaurants, cafes, and nightlife
5. **Fremont Sunday Market** - Vintage finds and artisan food vendors
